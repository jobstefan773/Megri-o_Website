2001
Spirited Away
★ Best Animated Feature Film
This follows the story of Chihiro, a young girl who gets trapped in a mystical spirit world while moving to a new neighborhood with her parents. To save her family, she must work at a bathhouse owned by the powerful witch Yubaba. Along the way, Chihiro encounters various spirits, learns valuable lessons, and discovers her inner strength. With the help of her friends, she confronts Yubaba and secures her family's freedom, ultimately returning to the human world.
1997
Princess Mononoke
★ Best Home Video Release
Ashitaka, a young warrior, becomes cursed while defending his village. He embarks on a journey to find a cure and becomes entangled in a conflict between industrial humans and nature. Led by Lady Eboshi and Princess Mononoke, respectively, the opposing sides clash over the forest's resources. Ashitaka seeks to broker peace amid the escalating tensions, grappling with themes of environmentalism and the consequences of human greed.
2004
Howl's Moving Castle
★ Outstanding Motion Picture
Sophie, a young woman cursed to live as an old lady, seeks a cure and encounters Howl, a wizard with a magical moving castle. As war threatens the kingdom, they embark on a journey filled with adventure, friendship, and love, ultimately discovering the transformative power of self-acceptance and compassion.
1988
My Neighbor Totoro
★ Best Genre Video Release
Two young sisters, Satsuki and Mei, move to the countryside with their father to be closer to their ailing mother. In their new rural home, they encounter Totoro, a friendly forest spirit, and embark on magical adventures together. As they navigate their mother's illness and the challenges of growing up, Totoro becomes a source of comfort and joy, helping them find solace in the wonders of nature and the bonds of family.
2008
Ponyo
★ Best Asian Film
Young Sosuke befriends a magical goldfish named Ponyo, whose desire to become human disrupts the ocean's balance. As they journey together, they encounter Ponyo's sorcerer father, Fujimoto, who struggles to control his daughter's magical abilities. Through their courage and determination, Sosuke and Ponyo navigate the wonders of the ocean, learning valuable lessons about friendship, responsibility, and the importance of preserving nature's harmony.
